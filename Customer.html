Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and more.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our Order Tracking page or on the courier company's website to check the status of your delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 3-4 days return policy for most items. The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the original invoice. Some items like perishable goods and personal care products may not be eligible for return.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 2-5 business days within India. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and product availability. You can check estimated delivery times during checkout.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working to expand our services internationally in the near future.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. We also offer Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible orders.

How can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order from your account page if it hasn't been shipped yet. For orders that have already been shipped, you can refuse delivery or return the item once received.
